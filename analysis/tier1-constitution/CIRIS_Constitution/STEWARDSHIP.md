# Stewardship

Stewarded by **Eric Moore**, founder.

This constitution carries no ratification date and no expiry. It is perpetually incomplete — it
simply *is*: a living document, never finished and never lapsed.

It may be amended by the founder, or by any accord-holder, as the work requires; and the document's
own governance may, over time, tighten that amendment authority — also as the work requires.
